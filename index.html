About Homegrown Spirits

We're a small art business run by Sullivan Steele and his partner out of Charleston, WV. It started with a sand-blasting cabinet and some pint glasses. It grew from there.

Every handmade piece is made by us. Some items are printed on demand through trusted services (Printful, Redbubble) so we can offer more variety without taking on more inventory. Digital items are delivered instantly.

Payments are processed securely through PayPal — we never see your card details. Custom orders welcome — just email.
